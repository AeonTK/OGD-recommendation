A recommender system for improved data findability in open government data portals

The research suggests that it is difficult for users of open government data (OGD) portals to find the datasets they are interested in. The objective of our research is to contribute to the solution of this problem by developing a recommendation system for datasets. This recommendation system will output possibly relevant datasets based on the chosen dataset. This survey is meant to evaluate the effectiveness of this recommendation method approach. Recommended datasets column contains metadata about the datasets that are recommended to user based upon the dataset under Main dataset column. We ask you to rate the quality of recommendation under the Voting column and send results to us with submit button which will redirect you to email results to us. Thank you!

These maps originate in 1826-1843 and originally were intended for archiving in the Central Archive of Cadastre of Lands in Vienna.
